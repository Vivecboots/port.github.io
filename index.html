About

Hi, I'm Blair Winslow-Nason. I'm a Junior Developer, residing in Seattle.

Since my teenage years, I have been deeply interested in programming and robotics. I taught myself C++ and read books on robotics in my spare time. When I discovered programs that could help me gain the skills and knowledge I needed, I enrolled in a sixteen-week, full-stack development program, through Generation USA. Additionally, I have earned the AWS Solutions Architect Certificate, the JavaScript W3 Certificate and Scrum Certificate. Through these programs I gained proficiency in a wide range of tools and technologies, and expanded my knowledge of networking and cloud computing. I am passionate about writing clean, efficient code that is easy for other developers to understand and maintain.
